Welcome to Caywood Ridge

Caywood Ridge is located in prestigious Oldham County. We are just outside of Crestwood, KY directly off of HWY KY 22, less than one mile past HWY 393.

At Caywood Ridge, you get the beauty and comfort of country living, with the convenience of city amenities just miles away. Historic Lagrange is just a few miles up the road, and a huge shopping area (The Paddock Shops) is less than 20 minutes away in Louisville.

How to find us:
From I-71 take exit 18 (HWY 393) and turn South on to 393
Go 2 miles until you reach HWY 22
Turn left onto HWY 22, Caywood Ridge will on the left in less than a mile

View Caywood Ridge on the Map



 

In addition to a prime location, Caywood Ridge also has access to the best school district in the state of Kentucky.

Elementary students attend Locust Grove Elementary, located just over a mile up Hwy 22 (adjacent to East Oldham Middle School). The school offers a comprehensive education with enrichment programs including the Lions' Pride After School Youth Enrichment Program. Your children will benefit from quality instruction and supportive learning opportunities at Locust Grove!

Middle school students attend East Oldham Middle School (EOMS), located just over a mile on Hwy 22. At EOMS, they strive to prepare students for academic success by providing a positive learning experience. Every EOMS student has amazing Patriotic Pride!

High school students will attend Oldham County High School (OCHS), which has been recognized twice as a National Blue Ribbon School of Excellence. OCHS strives to blend the latest research on learning and teaching strategies with traditional values that has been a strong foundation for our students for over 50 years.
